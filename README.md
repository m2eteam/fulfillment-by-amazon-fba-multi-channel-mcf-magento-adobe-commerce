# M2E Amazon MCF

M2E Amazon MCF is a trusted Adobe Commerce (Magento) extension that allows sellers to use Amazon FBA's advanced fulfillment network to ship orders across various sales channels.

With this extension, you can efficiently manage your inventory and fulfill orders not only from Amazon but also from other platforms, such as eBay, ensuring fast and reliable delivery to your customers.

Here's how else M2E Amazon MCF can benefit your business:

- Streamline your fulfillment process by automatically routing eBay orders to Amazon FBA
- Keep your Magento stock levels up-to-date with Amazon FBA inventory synch to prevent overselling
- Manage and track MCF orders and shipments directly from your Magento admin panel
- Reduce operational costs and improve buyer satisfaction with Amazon's efficient fulfillment network

Check out our [user documentation](https://docs-m2.m2epro.com/m2e-amazon-mcf-magento) to learn more about the extension.
